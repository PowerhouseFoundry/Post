Decision guide

What should I look for?

  1. Find the company name and the letter date.
  2. Look for words such as due, overdue, reminder, welcome, confirmation, direct debit, prize draw or final notice.
  3. Check whether there is an amount to pay now.
  4. If it is a new account, you may need to set up a direct debit instead of paying by card.
  5. Some post may be junk or a scam and can be ignored.
